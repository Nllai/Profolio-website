What did I learn

Through this project, I strengthened my understanding and confidence in building a new website from 0 to 100.

I learned that companies that need portfolios (such as construction companies) require a clear display of the entire service process and a beautiful display of portfolios. The specific skills are also reflected in BY Homes, a company of the same type that I made later, and the latter can be found in my Development portfolio.

I also gained experience of business negotiation and manage multiple tasks at the same time.
